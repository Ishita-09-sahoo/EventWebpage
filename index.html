About the Event

Guwahati Half Marathon is the biggest student conducted marathon conducted by the students of IIT GUWAHATI every year. This marathon is conducted not only for the purpose of running but also for a social cause. We collab with different NGO's across the city to contribute towards the cause. There are 2 types of marathon -

  • Spirit Run: It is 6km long. Participation is free for all.
  • Glory Run: It is 12km long. Participation is 500/- per head.

Schedule
  • Date - 6th April 2025
  • Time - 5:15am
  • Venue - Sarusajai Stadium, Guwahati
Theme

This year the theme is "Run for a Healthier Society". We will be conducting various awareness campaigns for chronic or fatal diseases like Cancer, AIDS and others. We will set up medical checkup camps and blood donation camps at the rural areas of Guwahati in collaboration with various NGO's. We will invite doctors to give awareness speeches about various health domains.
